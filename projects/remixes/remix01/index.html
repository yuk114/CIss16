Mirror, mirror, on the wall,

why does this world make me feel so small?

temporary highs, and again I fall,

while someone somewhere spins the ball,

glassy buildings and shiny malls,

models dressed up to enthrall,

doubts and fears, up they crawl,

I stare at you, through it all,

watching the shadows grow tall,

tell me when will the end befall?
